Steps involved in Accessings contacts

For Web Browsers
Step 1: Visit the contacts page
1.1: Enter the URL in the address bar of your web browser. For example, "https://example.com/contacts".
Step 2: Select the desired contact
2.1: Click on the contact's name or email address.
Alternative: Import the contacts
3.1: Go to the "Settings" or "Preferences" section in your web browser.
3.2: Find the "Contacts" or "Address Book" section.
3.3: Select "Import and Backup" or "Import Bookmarks".
3.4: Choose "From a file" and select the downloaded contacts file from your device.
